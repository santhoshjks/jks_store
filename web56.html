Bang & Olufsen Beoplay H95

Luxury over‑ear headphones with titanium drivers, adaptive ANC and up to 38 hours battery life with ANC.

BANG & OLUFSEN Beoplay H95

Beoplay H95 uses 40 mm titanium drivers with neodymium magnets, adaptive active noise cancellation and premium aluminium and leather materials to deliver detailed sound, strong isolation and long‑term comfort.

Technical Specifications

Drivers & Audio

40 mm electro‑dynamic titanium drivers with neodymium magnets, tuned for precise sound across 20 Hz to 22 kHz.

Sensitivity & Impedance

Sensitivity around 101.5 dB at 1 mW/1 kHz and impedance of about 12 Ω (±15%).

Noise Cancellation

Adaptive Active Noise Cancellation with adjustable levels plus transparency mode for hearing surroundings when needed.

Wireless & Codecs

Bluetooth 5.1 with support for SBC, AAC and aptX Adaptive along with multipoint pairing for two devices.

Battery & Charging

Up to about 38 hours playback with Bluetooth and ANC enabled and up to 50 hours with ANC off, charging in roughly 2–3 hours.

Controls & Mics

Touch controls plus aluminium dials on the earcups with several MEMS microphones dedicated to calls and noise cancelling.

Build & Comfort

Aluminium frame, lambskin memory‑foam ear cushions and cowhide leather headband for comfortable long listening sessions.

Dimensions & Weight

Over‑ear design around 185 × 165 × 80 mm and approximately 320 g, with a compact aluminium travel case included.

Accessories & Warranty

Comes with aluminium hard case, USB‑C cable, 3.5 mm audio cable, flight adapter and cloth, with warranty around 3 years in many regions.
