Sony WI-1000XM2 Wireless Noise‑Cancelling

Premium neckband headphones with industry‑leading noise cancellation, LDAC and up to 10 hours battery life.

Sony WI-1000XM2

₹63,861

Sony WI‑1000XM2 combines a comfortable flexible neckband with HD Noise Cancelling Processor QN1, LDAC codec support and DSEE HX upscaling for high‑resolution wireless audio on the go.

Technical Specifications

Drivers & Audio

Hybrid driver system with 9 mm dynamic driver and balanced armature, neodymium magnets and DSEE HX upscaling.

Frequency & Impedance

Frequency response from around 3 Hz to 40,000 Hz (wired) and impedance about 50 Ω in wired operation with noise cancelling on.

Noise cancelling

HD Noise Cancelling Processor QN1 with Dual Noise Sensor technology, ambient sound mode and noise cancelling on/off control.

Wireless & Codecs

Bluetooth 5.0 with NFC pairing and support for SBC, AAC and LDAC high‑resolution wireless audio codecs.

Battery & Charging

Up to about 10 hours playback with noise cancelling on and fast charge giving roughly 80 minutes from a 10‑minute charge via USB‑C.

Controls & Voice

Inline remote for playback, volume and noise control plus support for Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa voice assistants.

Design & Comfort

Lightweight neckband design around 58 g with flexible silicone band and magnetic earbuds for tangle‑free storage.

Included Accessories

Carry case, USB‑C charging cable, 3.5 mm audio cable, airplane adapter and multiple earbud tip sizes.
