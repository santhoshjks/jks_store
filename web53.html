Shure SE846 Pro In‑Ear

Quad‑driver sound‑isolating earphones with customizable sound signature and up to 37 dB passive isolation.

Shure SE846 features four high‑definition balanced‑armature drivers, a low‑pass filter for subwoofer‑like bass, and interchangeable nozzles so listeners can tune the sound to balanced, warm or bright profiles.

Technical Specifications

Drivers & Design

Four custom balanced‑armature drivers in a three‑way configuration with separate low, mid and high frequency paths.

Frequency & Sensitivity

Frequency range around 15 Hz to 20 kHz with sensitivity about 114 dB SPL per mW at 1 kHz.

Impedance & Isolation

Nominal impedance roughly 9 Ω at 1 kHz and passive sound isolation up to about 37 dB with supplied sleeves.

Custom Sound

Interchangeable nozzle inserts allow different signatures such as balanced, warm or bright using included filters and tool.

Cables & Connectivity

Detachable MMCX cables with over‑ear wireform fit, including standard audio cable and optional inline or wireless accessories.

Accessories

Fit kit with multiple silicone and foam sleeves, nozzle filters, airline and 6.3 mm adapters, case and cleaning tools.

Build & Warranty

Durable, sweat‑resistant housings with low‑profile over‑ear design and a limited manufacturer warranty of about two years.
