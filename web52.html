Denon PerL Pro uses Masimo Adaptive Acoustic Technology to create a personalized hearing profile, with 10 mm triple‑layer titanium drivers, aptX Lossless, adaptive ANC and up to 8 hours of playback per charge.

Technical Specifications

Personalized Sound

Masimo Adaptive Acoustic Technology (AAT) measures hearing and optimizes sound for each ear.

Drivers & Range

10 mm triple‑layer titanium dynamic drivers with frequency range roughly 20 Hz to 40 kHz for detailed audio.

Noise cancelling

Adaptive active noise cancellation with adjustable transparency or social mode to hear surroundings when needed.

Codecs & Spatial

Support for aptX Lossless, aptX Adaptive, AAC and SBC, plus Dirac Virtuo spatial audio for immersive stereo.

Wireless & Battery

Bluetooth 5.3 with multipoint, around 8 hours per earbud and up to about 24–32 hours with the charging case, including quick charge.

Charging

USB‑C wired fast charging and Qi wireless charging case for convenient top‑ups.

Calls & Mics

Four microphones per earbud including bone‑conduction mics for clearer voice pickup and aptX Voice support on calls.

Controls & App

Customizable touch controls with Denon app, 5‑band EQ and multiple sound presets.

Comfort & Durability

Multiple ear tip sizes and stabilizing wings plus IPX4 water‑resistant design for sweat and rain.

Dimensions

Compact earbuds and charging case sized to be pocket friendly while holding several extra charges.
