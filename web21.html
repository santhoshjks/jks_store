Experience the future with Vivo X200 Pro, featuring a stunning 6.78" Quad-curved LTPO AMOLED display, powerful MediaTek Dimensity 9400 chipset, and professional-grade camera system with ZEISS optics.

Technical Specifications

Display

6.78″ Quad‑curved LTPO AMOLED (1260 × 2800 px FHD+, ~452 ppi), 120 Hz adaptive refresh, HDR10+, Dolby Vision, peak brightness 4,500 nits (0.1‑120 Hz)

Chipset

MediaTek Dimensity 9400 (3 nm), octa‑core CPU: 1×3.63 GHz Cortex‑X925 + 3×3.3 GHz Cortex‑X4 + 4×2.4 GHz Cortex‑A720; GPU: Immortalis‑G925

Memory & Storage

16 GB LPDDR5X RAM; 512 GB or 1 TB UFS 4.0 storage; no expandable storage

Operating System

Android 15 with Funtouch OS 15 (OriginOS 5 in select regions)

Rear Cameras

Triple-camera system with ZEISS optics: 50 MP main (Sony LYT‑818, f/1.57, OIS, PDAF), 50 MP ultra-wide (f/2.0, autofocus, 119° FoV), 200 MP periscope telephoto (ISOCELL HP9 sensor, f/2.67, OIS, 3.7× optical zoom, macro support)

Front Camera

32 MP (f/2.0, ultra‑wide lens), supports 4K video recording at up to 60 fps

Video

Rear: 8K @ 30 fps, 4K up to 120 fps (10‑bit HDR, gyro-EIS, Cinematic Portrait); Front: 4K @ 60 fps, 1080p @ 60 fps

Battery

6,000 mAh (typical, global); some European variants use 5,200 mAh; supports 90 W wired FlashCharge, 30 W wireless charging, reverse wired charging; semi-solid silicon‑anode battery rated to operate down to –20 °C

Build & Durability

Dimensions ~162.36 × 75.95 × 8.2–8.49 mm; weight ~223–228 g; IP68 & IP69 water/dust resistance; glass or glass‑fiber back, premium metal frame; ultrasonic under‑display fingerprint sensor; stereo speakers; optional satellite connectivity support

Connectivity

5G (Sub‑6 & mmWave), dual SIM (Nano), Wi‑Fi 6/7, Bluetooth 5.4 (aptX HD, LHDC), NFC, infrared blaster, USB‑C 3.2 Gen1 (OTG), GPS (A-GPS, BeiDou, GLONASS, Galileo, NavIC), OTG, Bluetooth, no headphone jack

Extras & Imaging

V3+ imaging chip for advanced AI processing; Zeiss T* lens coatings; second‑generation framing features like Full Focal‑Range 4K LOG video, telephoto macro, SuperLandscape, Portrait Video; high‑frequency PWM dimming display optimized for eye care
